This video is a compilation of projects that I produced in my advanced animation class. Project Summary

The Force

A personal project that I did to learn how to rig and skin a mesh while also utilizing what I learned from my lightsaber render to demonstrate my understanding of lighting and emission. It features an animated lightsaber and hand with a functioning rig that catches and grabs the lightsaber as it is force pulled to itself. This was produced in 3DS Max.
